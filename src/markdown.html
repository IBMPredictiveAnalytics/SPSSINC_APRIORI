SPSSINC APRIORI Extension Command

Discover association rules in a dataset. Association rules are statements of the form:

if antecedent(s) then consequent(s)

SPSSINC APRIORI varlist*

/CRITERIA SUPPORT=value CONFIDENCE=value MINLIFT=1.0

/FORMAT SUPPORT =A or D CONFIDENCE=A or D LIFT=A or D MAXRULES=number

/OPTIONS ITEMPLOT=YES or NO** SUMMARY=YES or NO**

/HELP

* Required
**Default
